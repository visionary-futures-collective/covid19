#COVIDCampus news corpus

The VFC is collecting news articles related broadly to the topic of coronavirus on college campuses. Our purpose is to make visible the shifting discourse around campus closings and reopenings, and the ways that life, labor, and economics are manipulated in the name of access to higher education.

Want to contribute? See our documentation for adding to the corpus.

#COVIDCampus FeelingsMap

The FeelingsMap will iteratively collect emotion-based responses to campus and national news to visually represent our collective moment. Our intention is to provide an ever-developing map of feelings across cities, regions, and campuses, and in return for submissions, provide ways individuals can engage with VFC project development.

#COVIDCampus visualizations

The VFC will be designing and publishing data visualizations with the goal of informing conversations about upcoming semester plans. Our intention is to visualize data that can have an impact on how we approach and manage the Fall 2020 semester.
